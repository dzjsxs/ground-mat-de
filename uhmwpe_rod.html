Product Introduction

UHMWPE Rod and Bar stock provides the raw material for machining high-performance wear components, bearings, and structural parts in demanding industrial applications. With the same exceptional properties as UHMWPE sheet — outstanding wear resistance, high impact strength, self-lubrication, and universal chemical resistance — UHMWPE rod enables the production of complex three-dimensional components through conventional machining operations.

UHMWPE rod is produced through ram extrusion, resulting in a uniform, dense structure with consistent properties throughout the cross-section. Its excellent machinability allows for tight tolerances and smooth surface finishes without the need for specialized tooling. The material's low coefficient of friction eliminates the need for external lubrication in most applications, reducing maintenance costs and contamination risks.
