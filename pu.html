Product Introduction

Polyurethane (PU) Sheet combines the elasticity of rubber with the toughness of engineering plastics, offering a unique material profile that fills the performance gap between conventional rubbers and rigid plastics. PU sheet exhibits exceptional abrasion resistance — up to 10 times greater than natural rubber — combined with high load-bearing capacity, excellent tear resistance, and good chemical resistance.

PU sheet is available in a wide range of hardness values (20 Shore A to 75 Shore D), allowing engineers to select the optimal balance of flexibility and stiffness for each application. Its excellent resilience (high energy return) makes it ideal for impact-absorbing applications, while its oil resistance and ozone resistance provide durability in harsh industrial environments.
