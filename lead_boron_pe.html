Product Introduction

Lead-Boron Polyethylene Sheet is a specialized composite material engineered for nuclear radiation shielding applications. By incorporating lead powder and boron compounds into a high-density polyethylene matrix, this material provides effective attenuation of gamma rays (lead component), thermal neutrons (boron component), and fast neutrons (hydrogen-rich PE matrix) in a single, lightweight panel.

Compared to traditional lead sheet or concrete shielding, lead-boron PE offers significant advantages: it is lighter, easier to machine and fabricate, non-toxic in its solid form, and can be produced in complex shapes. The material is widely used in nuclear power plants, medical radiation therapy facilities, industrial radiography, and research reactors where comprehensive radiation protection is required.
